Kalpvruksha Smart GR System (RAG Architecture Chatbot)

AI-powered Chatbot for Understanding and Implementing Government Resolutions

View GitHub View Notion

Project Overview

Description: The Kalpvruksha Smart GR System is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture-based chatbot designed to simplify the understanding and implementation of Government Resolutions (GR). It enables users to quickly access and comprehend complex government regulations and policies through an interactive, AI-powered chat interface.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered chatbot for easy interaction with government resolutions.
  • Utilizes Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture for accurate and context-aware responses.
  • Real-time access to a comprehensive database of government resolutions and policies.
  • Interactive chat interface for better user engagement and understanding.
  • Seamless integration with government databases for up-to-date information.

Target Audience: Government employees, policy implementers, and individuals seeking to better understand and navigate government regulations.

Role: Project Lead – Led the entire project lifecycle, from initial planning and requirement gathering to full deployment and stakeholder communication.

Technology: React, FastAPI, RAG Architecture, Natural Language Processing (NLP), AI-driven Chatbot
