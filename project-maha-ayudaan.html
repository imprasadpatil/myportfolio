Mahaayudaan Digital Health Mission

Empowering Organ Donation & Transplantation in Maharashtra

View GitHub View Notion

Project Overview

Description: Mahaayudaan is a state-of-the-art digital platform aimed at improving organ donation and transplantation processes across Maharashtra. It provides a streamlined system for registering organ pledges, managing transplant data, and ensuring transparency in organ allocation.

Key Features:

  • Easy registration for individuals pledging to donate organs.
  • Comprehensive database management for organ donors and transplant recipients.
  • Real-time updates on potential cadaver donors in hospitals.
  • Coordination between hospitals and health authorities for efficient organ allocation.
  • Alignment with national policies and guidelines, ensuring compliance.

Target Audience: Hospitals, Zonal Transplant Coordination Committees (ZTCCs), health authorities, and individuals looking to pledge their organs for donation.

Role: Project Developer – Lead the development of the Mahaayudaan platform, ensuring seamless integration of donation registration, data management, and coordination features.

Technology: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, React.js, JavaScript, REST APIs, Cloud Hosting, Secure Data Handling
